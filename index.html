About Akira

Akira (1988), directed by Katsuhiro Otomo, is a seminal cyberpunk anime set in a dystopian Neo-Tokyo, 2019, after World War III. The story follows Shōtarō Kaneda, a biker gang leader, as his friend Tetsuo Shima gains uncontrollable telekinetic powers from a covert government experiment. Renowned for its stunning animation, neon-drenched visuals, and intense action, Akira explores themes of power, corruption, and societal decay. Its iconic bike slide, with Kaneda’s red motorcycle, is a cultural touchstone. Akira revolutionized anime’s global influence, blending raw energy with profound philosophical questions, cementing its legacy as a timeless masterpiece.
